Modeling complex systems· Ideas.
 
   

      I work on understanding complex systems — how they behave, how we model them,       and where models break down.    

   

      My research focuses on hydropower and power system dynamics.    

   

      Alongside this, I write — mostly to think. About modeling, uncertainty, and how technical systems interact with the real world.    

 
 

Research

 
   
System Identification of Swing Dynamics
   
      Estimating inertia and damping in nonlinear power system models, with focus on realistic disturbances and measurement noise.
 
   
Nonlinear Modeling in Power Systems
   
      Exploring limitations of linear approximations and developing identification approaches for strongly nonlinear dynamics.    
 
 

Projects

 
   
Hydropower System Modeling
   
      Simulation and modeling of turbine and generator behavior for stability and control analysis.    
 
 
   
System Identification Experiments
   
      Working with real and synthetic data to estimate dynamic parameters under noise and uncertainty.
